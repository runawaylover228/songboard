Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: Songboard does not operate a server and does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to us. Everything you do on Songboard stays in your own browser. We have no user database.

1. Who We Are

Songboard ("we", "us", "our") is a music social platform operated by Runawaylover228. You can contact us about privacy matters at: songboard-privacy@proton.me (placeholder — update before launch).

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to all visitors and users of Songboard, accessible at https://runawaylover228.github.io/songboard.

Songboard is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly permit anyone under the age of 18 to use this service.

3. Data We Collect and How

3a. Data stored only on your device (localStorage)

The following information is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Your display name, username, and bio
  • Your chosen avatar colour
  • Posts, comments, and likes you create
  • Your age verification status (stored as "verified" or "blocked" — your exact birth year is never saved)

This data exists only on your device. We have no access to it. If you clear your browser storage, it is permanently deleted.

3b. Data sent to third-party services

When you search for a song, your search query is sent to Apple's iTunes Search API (itunes.apple.com). This is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not control what Apple does with search queries.

The Apple Music embed player (embed.music.apple.com) may set its own cookies or collect usage data when you interact with it. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

3c. Hosting

Songboard is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). GitHub may collect server logs including your IP address when you visit the site. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

3d. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not use cookies of our own
  • We do not use analytics trackers (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, etc.)
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information
  • We do not run advertisements
  • We do not sell data — we have no data to sell

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • Songboard is not directed at anyone under 18.
  • We ask all new users to confirm their age before accessing the platform. Users who indicate they are under 18 are denied access.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.
  • If you believe someone under 18 has accessed or used Songboard, please contact us immediately at songboard-privacy@proton.me and we will take appropriate action.

Important: Our current age verification is a good-faith self-declaration. It is not a technically verified age check. We are working toward integrating a COPPA-compliant age verification solution. Until then, we rely on users and parents to report underage access.

5. Your Rights

Because we store no data server-side, most data rights are exercisable directly by you:

  • Access / Portability: Your data is in your browser's localStorage. You can inspect it at any time via your browser's developer tools.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's localStorage or site data to permanently delete all Songboard data from your device.
  • Correction: You can edit your profile at any time within the app.

If you are a resident of the European Union or California, and you believe any third party (Apple, GitHub) has processed your data in violation of applicable law, please contact those services directly or reach out to us and we will assist where possible.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data passes through our systems, there is no server-side risk of a data breach affecting your information. Your data is as secure as your own device and browser.

We do enforce a Content Security Policy (CSP) on all pages to prevent cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks, and all user-generated content is HTML-escaped before display.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of Songboard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or to report underage access, contact us at:
songboard-privacy@proton.me (update before launch)
