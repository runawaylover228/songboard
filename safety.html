Who Can Use Songboard

Songboard is only for people aged 18 and older. This is a firm requirement, not a suggestion. Anyone under 18 who attempts to join is blocked from the platform entirely.

If you are a parent and believe your child under 18 has accessed Songboard, please email songboard-safety@proton.me immediately.

Our Commitments

  • No data collection from minors. We do not collect personal data from users under 18.
  • Zero tolerance for CSAM. Any child sexual abuse material is immediately reported to NCMEC and law enforcement.
  • No advertising targeting. We run no ads, and we never use your data for targeting of any kind.
  • No dark patterns. We do not use manipulative design to keep you on the platform longer than you want to be.
  • Transparency. Our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service are written in plain language.

Staying Safe Online

🔒

Protect your identity

Don't share your real name, school, location, or phone number in your profile or posts.

🚩

Trust your instincts

If something feels wrong or uncomfortable, it probably is. Report it and step away.

💬

Talk to someone you trust

If someone online makes you uncomfortable, tell a parent, guardian, or trusted adult.

🛑

Never meet strangers alone

Never agree to meet someone in person that you only know from online, especially alone.
